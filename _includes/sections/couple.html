David and Elise

Elise Blasingame, Atlanta native, and David Torello, not from Atlanta, met when Beyonce took down the Superdome during the 2013 Superbowl. Elise tried to flee to Africa, but Torellomagnetism is a powerful force and she could not escape its pull. Soon followed the dog, then the house, then the chickens. The rest is yet to be written!
