Wedding Accommodations

We have a few recommendations, but we are not booking a hotel block for the wedding. We recommend staying somewhere near downtown Atlanta or in an Airbnb location within our neighborhood of Adair Park (or close by). The wedding will take place in the West End neighborhood, which is right next to Adair Park in southwest Atlanta.

If you want to stay at a hotel, here are some recommendations in no particular order:

Atlanta Marriott Marquis Downtown
This is where Hunger Games was filmed and where DragonCon takes place every year!

Hilton Garden Inn Atlanta Downtown

Omni Atlanta Hotel at CNN Center

Glenn Hotel, Autograph Collection

W Hotel Downtown

Wildcard: Hotel Clermont
Recently renovated historic Atlanta landmark. Boutique hotel nestled in the heart of Atlanta. Elise and David are staying here.
